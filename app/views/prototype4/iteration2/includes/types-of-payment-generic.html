Paying and receiving child maintenance

If you choose to use the Child Maintenance Service there are 2 ways payments can be made.

Direct Pay

The paying parent will pay maintenance directly to the receiving parentâ€™s bank account.

You do not need to have any contact with the other parent and there are no additional fees.

Collect and Pay

The Child Maintenance Service will collect maintenance from the paying parent and pass it to the receiving parent.

There are additional fees for you and the other parent and you can only use Collect and Pay if the paying parent agrees to it or does not respond to an application.
