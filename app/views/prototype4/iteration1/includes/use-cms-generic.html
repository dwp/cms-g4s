The Child Maintenance Service may be able to arrange child maintenance for you. This is less flexible than making your own arrangement because the calculation is a fixed amount based on the paying parent’s earnings.

Using the Child Maintenance Service might be a good option for if you do not feel safe dealing with the other parent, or you do not think they will stick to an agreement.

The Child Maintenance Service will:

  • work out how much child maintenance will be paid
  • arrange for payments to be made between the parents
  • take action if payments are not made
  • help find the other parent if you’ve lost contact
  • recalculate the payment amount if changes in parents’ circumstances are reported
  • help if someone denies they’re the parent of a child
