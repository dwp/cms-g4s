Paying child maintenance

If you choose to use the Child Maintenance Service there are 2 ways you can make payments

Direct Pay

You pay maintenance directly into the other parent’s bank account.

You do not need to have any contact with the other parent and there are no additional fees.

Collect and Pay

The Child Maintenance Service will collect maintenance from you and pass it to the other parent.

There are additional fees for you and the other parent.

Receiving child maintenance

If you choose to use the Child Maintenance Service there are 2 ways you can get payments

Direct Pay

The other parent will pay maintenance directly to your bank account.

You do not need to have any contact with the other parent and there are no additional fees.

Collect and Pay

The Child Maintenance Service will collect maintenance from the other parent and pass it to you.

There are additional fees for you and the other parent and you can only use Collect and Pay if the other parent agrees to it or does not respond to the application.
