Domestic abuse

We need to ask you a question about domestic abuse so we can show you where to get help if you need it. Also, you may not have to pay the £20 application fee if you have experienced domestic abuse.

Have you or your children experienced any of these behaviours?

  • psychological or emotional abuse
  • physical or sexual abuse
  • financial or economic abuse
  • coercive control (an act or pattern of acts of assault, threats, humiliation, intimidation, degradaton, isolation and control)
  • harrassment and stalking
  • online or digital abuse
