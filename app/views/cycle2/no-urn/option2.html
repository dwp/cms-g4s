Your options

Use the Child Maintenance Service

You can use the Child Maintenance Service to arrange child maintenance for you. This is less flexible than making your own arrangement because they will decide how much maintenance will be paid.

Using the Child Maintenance Service might be a good option for if you don't feel safe dealing with the other parent, or you don't think they will pay.

The Child Maintenance Service will:

  • work out child maintenance payment amounts
  • arrange for the other parent to pay
  • take action if payments are not made
  • help find the other parent if you've lost contact
  • look at the payments if changes in parents’ circumstances are reported
  • help if someone denies they’re the parent of a child
